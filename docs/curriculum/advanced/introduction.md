# Introduction to Advanced Topics

## Required Hours

**60 hours** (part of Core Curriculum: 240 hours)

## Session Pattern

- Lecture and discussion: 20 hours
- Advanced labs: 25 hours
- Research and exploration: 10 hours
- Assessment: 5 hours

## Purpose

The Advanced Topics module expands student knowledge into specialized areas, building on core development skills. Students explore cutting-edge technologies and advanced concepts relevant to their field of study.

## Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this module, students will be able to:

1. Apply advanced concepts to complex problems
2. Evaluate and select appropriate technologies for specific use cases
3. Design scalable and maintainable systems
4. Research and learn new technologies independently
5. Contribute to advanced technical discussions

## Deliverables

Students will complete the following deliverables:

1. **Advanced Technical Project** - Complex application using advanced concepts
2. **Technology Research Report** - In-depth analysis of emerging technology
3. **Architecture Design Document** - Comprehensive system design
4. **Technical Presentation** - Knowledge sharing with peers

## Assessment Criteria

Evaluation based on:

- Complexity and quality of technical project
- Depth of research and analysis
- Quality of architecture and design decisions
- Presentation effectiveness and knowledge sharing
